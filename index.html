The State Page Network  ·  Two Live Deployments

A multi-deployment civic news platform.

I designed and shipped The State Page Network: a multi-deployment civic news aggregation platform with production AI editorial pipelines, now live in two structurally different markets. As product owner and editorial architect, I made every brand, editorial, source curation, section structure, and architectural decision. I wrote the editorial prompt architecture for both deployments and directed AI-assisted development end to end.

Both sites run shared architectural DNA, Next.js 14 App Router, Vercel ISR with five-minute revalidation, Cloudflare DNS, Gemini 2.5 Flash editorial AI, dual-typeface system, adapted at every layer to the realities of each market. Building the second deployment is what revealed which parts of the first were genuinely shared infrastructure and which were market-specific assumptions. Adapting to Texas required deliberate new decisions at almost every layer: brand identity and color, section structure (seven down to four), source list, language polarity, agenda strip logic, and a rewritten categorization prompt with a Texas relevance gate.
